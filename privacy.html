Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

1. Overview

HomeStyle Curator ("the App") is a personal content scheduling tool. We do not collect, store, or share personal data from third parties.

2. Data We Access

The App accesses Pinterest account data solely to create and publish pins on behalf of the authenticated user. No data is shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the developer's machine. No external databases or cloud services store user data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the Pinterest API under Pinterest's terms of service. No other third-party data processors are used.
